Admissions

The steps to securing a spot for a NEW student at our open-enrollment charter are to apply, enroll and register. Students that wish to return for the next school year should not fill out a OneApp application. Completing a OneApp application could result in the loss of the student's seat at Peruser.

Apply

To apply, complete an OneApp application. If placed at Peruser, you will then receive a seat acceptance letter with registration instructions from our portal.

Enroll

To enroll the student has to be placed at Peruser by OneApp. An enroll code will be sent to families from OneAppwith instructions on how to enroll and register.

Register

Once you have confirmed your child's placement at Peruser, you will receive a confirmation email with a link to complete registration. Assistance with online registration is available, if needed.

Important Dates

Main round opens November.

Required Documents for Registration

OneApp placement results will be made available by our portal on the parent application portal. To register at the school by following the instructions below after accepting the student's placement. Even if you have accepted your seat in the OneApp portal, you will not be registered at Peruser without completing this enrollment procedure.

  • Copy of student Birth Certificate
  • Copy of final report card
  • Copy of immunization records
  • Copy of available standardized test scores
  • Copy of Driver's License of parent or legal representative
  • Official copy of student transcripts
  • Proof of legal custody if the guardians are not the biological parents of the child(ren).
  • Three proofs of residency.
